Decline Sit-Ups w/ Twist

(3 sets |15, 12, 10)

Info: There will be 3 sets. Secure your feet into a decline bench and perform a sit up to target the abs with a twist at the top to target the obliques. Lower the amount of reps each set.

Form: Find a decline bench and secure your feet in the padding. Perform a sit-up, arching your back and then bending at the abdominals to sit up. At the top, twist to your left and then to your right in order to engage the obliques and then lower yourself back to starting position.

Hanging Leg Raises

(3 sets |15, 15, 15)

*To make it easier, only lift the knees upward*

Info: There will be 3 sets. While hanging from a pullup bar, lift your legs outward to target the lower abs. Keep the reps the same throughout.

Form: Hang from a bar and slightly bend your arms and engage your lats in order to stay in place on the bar and not swing. Lift your legs upward and point them straight out, bending at the abdominals.

Sit-Ups X Leg Raises

(3 sets |10x10, 10x10, 10x10)

Info: There will be 3 sets. Perform the sit-ups first and immediately follow them with leg raises. Keep reps constant throughout.

Form: For the sit ups lay on the floor but bend your knees. Arch your back and then sit up bending at the abdominals.

For the leg raises, lay flat on the ground with your feet together. Keeping your hands on your abs, lift your legs up and point them straight in the air. When they are straight lift your glutes off the ground using your core.
