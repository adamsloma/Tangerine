StairMaster

Fat Loss

5 minute warm up period: climb at a normal pace

Fat Loss Interval: Perform between 10 and 20 times for a workout.
- Fast climbing period: 1 minutes of moderate speed stepping focusing on leg contraction
- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense stepping focusing on getting as many steps as possible

Fat Loss Interval x 10 = 25 minutes
Fat Loss Interval x 20 = 45 minutes

Conditioning

5 minute warm up period: climb at a normal pace

Conditioning Interval: Perform between 15 and 25 times for a workout.
- Fast climbing period: 2 minutes of moderate speed stepping focusing on leg contraction
- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense stepping focusing on getting as many steps as possible

Conditioning Interval x 10 = 35 minutes
Conditioning Interval x 20 = 65 minutes
