Legs 101

Squats

(5 sets | 15, 15, 12, 12, 10)

Info: There will be 5 sets. Start with a weight you can focus on control for higher repetitions and increase the weight 10-15% the following each set. For sets 2 and 4, increase the weight but attempt to complete the same amount of repetitions.

Form: Place your feet just outside of a normal standing position. Point your toes at about a 30 degree angle outward. With an arch in your back, sit back so that the bar travels in a straight line as you squat. Lower yourself all the way until your glutes nearly touch your ankles. Make sure that your heels do not come off the ground and that you stay flat footed. Push through your heels to bring yourself back upward.

Barbell Lunges

(4 sets | 12, 12, 10, 10)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Start with a weight you can focus on control for higher repetitions. After 2 sets, increase the weight 10-15% and lower the repetitions for the remaining 2 sets.

Form: With a barbell on your back, take one step back so that you are able to lower yourself so that your back knee touches the ground. Step back far enough so that your shin stays straight. Using your quads, return yourself so that both feet are next to each other. Repeat on the other leg to complete one rep.

Hamstring Curls

(4 sets | 12, 12, 10, 10)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Start with a weight you can focus on control for higher repetitions. After 2 sets, increase the weight 10-15% and lower the repetitions for the remaining 2 sets.

Form: On a hamstring curl machine, orientate yourself so that the padding is right above your achilles. Keeping your legs still, curl the weight so that it focuses on your hamstring. Lower it back to the starting position slowly.

Calf Raises

(4 sets | 15, 15, 12, 12)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Start with a weight you can focus on control for higher repetitions. After 2 sets, increase the weight 10-15% and lower the repetitions for the remaining 2 sets.
