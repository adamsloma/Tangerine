Flat Barbell Bench Press

(4 sets | 15, 12, 12, 10-8)

Info: There will be 4 sets. The first three sets will be focused on high repetitions and good form. Increase the weight about 15-20% after the first set. For the last set, increase the weight another 15-20%. This will be a drop set where after completing the first 10 reps, you will drop the weight about 30-45% and complete 8 more reps.

Form: Grab the bar just outside of shoulder width. Draw your shoulder blades backward. Bring the bar in a straight down motion and touch the bar to your chest around your sternum. Keep arms at about a 45 degree angle from your sides in order to keep pressure off your shoulders.

Incline Barbell Bench Press

(4 sets | 15, 12, 12, 10-8)

Info: There will be 4 sets. The first three sets will be focused on high repetitions and good form. The last set will be a drop set where after completing the first 10 reps, you will drop the weight about 30% and complete 8 more reps.

Form: Grab the bar just outside of shoulder width. Draw your shoulder blades backward. Bring the bar in a straight down motion and touch the bar to your chest around the upper part of your chest. Keep arms at about a 45 degree angle from your sides in order to keep pressure off your shoulders.


Incline Dumbbell Bench Press

(4 sets | 15, 15, 12, 12)

Info: There will be 4 sets. All 4 will be focused on high repetitions with an emphasis on good form. Increase the weight after the second set about 10-15%.

Form: Grab the dumbells and hold them above you touching. Draw your shoulder blades backward. Bring the bar in a straight down motion and bend your elbows just past 90 degrees. Keep arms at about a 45 degree angle from your sides in order to keep pressure off your shoulders.

Bodyweight Dips

(2 sets | AMRAP)

Info: There will be 2 sets. Both will be focused on burning out your muscles and performing as many reps as possible with good form.

Form: Grab the bars and hold yourself straight up. Lean forward slightly and lower yourself so that your arms bend to about 90 degrees.


Push-Ups

(1 set | AMRAP)

Info: There will be 1 set. It will focus on taking your chest to complete exhaustion. Perform as many reps as possible. If you don’t have enough energy to perform standard push-ups, do push-ups on your knees.

Form: Put your arms just outside shoulder width and keep a straight back. Lower yourself to the ground and push yourself back up.
