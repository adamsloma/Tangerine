Fat Loss

*Actively push/pull with your arms for best results*

(5 minute warm up period: move at a normal pace)

Fat Loss Interval: Perform between 10 and 20 times for a workout.

- Fast movement period: 2 minutes of fast movement

- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense movement

Fat Loss Interval x 10 = 35 minutes

Fat Loss Interval x 20 = 65 minutes

Conditioning

(10 minute warm up period: pedal at a light speed)

Conditioning Interval: Perform between 15 and 25 times for a workout.

- Fast movement period: 3 minutes of fast movement

- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense movement

Conditioning Interval x 10 = 50 minutes

Conditioning Interval x 20 = 90 minutes
