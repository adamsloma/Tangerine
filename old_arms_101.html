Arms 101

Close Grip Barbell Bench Press

(4 sets |15, 12, 10, 8)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Focus on control and contraction. Increase the weight and decrease the reps each set. 2-count negative, 1-count up, and 1-count squeeze will yield the best results.

Form: Grab the bar so that your hands are 4-5 inches apart. Draw your shoulder blades backward. Bring the bar in a straight down motion and touch the bar to your chest around your sternum. Keep your arms as close to your sides as possible in order to focus the triceps.

Barbell Curls

(4 sets |15, 12, 10, 8)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Focus on control and contraction. Increase the weight and decrease the reps each set. Focus on a 3-count negative and squeezing the muscle at the top.

Form: Grab the bar about shoulder width apart with your palms facing outward. Keeping your elbows at your sides, bend at the elbow and bring the bar toward your chest and squeeze the bicep at the top.

EZ-Bar Skullcrusher

(4 sets |15, 12, 10, 10)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Focus on control and contraction. Increase the weight and decrease the reps each set. Alternate arms while performing the set.

Form: Grab the dumbells and hold them at your sides with your palms facing each other. Keeping your elbow at your side, curl the bar up and keep your palms facing each other.

Dumbbell Hammer Curls

(4 sets |12, 12, 10, 10)

Info: There will be 4 sets. Focus on control and contraction. Increase the weight and decrease the reps each set. Alternate arms while performing the set.

Form: Grab the dumbells and hold them at your sides with your palms facing each other. Keeping your elbow at your side, curl the bar up and keep your palms facing each other.

Dumbbell Curls X Tricep Pushdowns

(2 sets | AMRAP)

Info: There will be 3 sets. Focus on control and contraction. Increase the weight and decrease the reps each set. Focus on a 3-count negative and squeezing the muscle at the top.

Form: For the dumbbell curls, hold the dumbbells at your sides with your palms facing forward. Keeping your elbows still, curl the dumbbell upward and at the top turn your hand slightly more outward to squeeze the bicep.

For the tricep pushdowns, attach a straight bar to the cable and grab it with your palms facing downward. Keeping your elbows at your sides and holding the bar straight in front of you, push the bar downward to fully extend your arm and squeeze the triceps.
