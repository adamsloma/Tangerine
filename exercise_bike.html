Fat Loss

5 minute warm up period: bike at a normal pace

Fat Loss Interval: Perform between 10 and 20 times for a workout.
- Fast pedaling period: 1 minutes of moderate speed pedaling
- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense/fast pedaling

Fat Loss Interval x 10 = 25 minutes
Fat Loss Interval x 20 = 45 minutes

Conditioning

10 minute warm up period: pedal at a light speed

Conditioning Interval: Perform between 15 and 25 times for a workout.
- Fast pedal period: 3 minutes of moderate speed pedaling
- Sprinting period: 1 minute of intense/fast pedaling

Conditioning Interval x 10 = 50 minutes
Conditioning Interval x 20 = 90 minutes
