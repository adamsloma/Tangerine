Rowing Machine

Fat Loss

5 minute warm up period: 2 minutes using just your arms to warm up the back, and 3 minutes doing the full movement at a normal pace

Fat Loss Interval: Perform between 10 and 20 times for a workout.
- Moderate rowing period: 1 minutes of moderate speed rowing
- Fast rowing period: 30 seconds of intense rowing

Fat Loss Interval x 10 = 20 minutes
Fat Loss Interval x 20 = 35 minutes

Conditioning

5 minute warm up period: 2 minutes using just your arms to warm up the back, and 3 minutes doing the full movement at a normal pace

Conditioning Interval: Perform between 15 and 25 times for a workout.
- Moderate rowing period: 2 minutes of moderate speed rowing
- Fast rowing period: 30 seconds of intense rowing

Conditioning Interval x 10 = 30 minutes
Conditioning Interval x 20 = 55 minutes
